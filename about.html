About me

Hi! My name is Logan and I'm currently a University of North Texas student working on a bachelor's in computer science which I will complete in May of 2024. Originally, I didn't go into college as a CS major, but when I took my first computer science class I fell in love with coding. My following semester I decided that I would enjoy my professional career much more as a developer rather than as an electrical engineer, so I made the switch and have been spending long days staring at my computer screen ever since.

I always have some ideas in my head about what I work on in my free time next, and once I get into the groove, my personal projects are all I want to work on when I've finished my work for the day.
