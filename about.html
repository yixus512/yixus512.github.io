About me

Hi! My name is Logan and I'm currently a University of North Texas student working on a bachelor's in computer science which I will complete in May of 2024. Originally, I didn't go into college as a CS major. I started out as a music major, but switched to electrical engineering after I decided I wanted to keep music as a personal hobby. This went without a hitch until I took my first computer science class and I fell in love with coding. My following semester I decided that I would enjoy my professional career much more as a developer rather than as an electrical engineer, so I made the switch and have been spending long days staring at my computer screen ever since.

Skillset

Primarily, my coding vocabulary is in object oriented languages like Python, C/C++, and Java. I also have experience with SQL and AWS, and I have taken classes on networking and cybersecurity.
